Install Office 365 or Office 2016

  1. Go to  www.office.com and if you're not already signed in, select  Sign in.
  1. Sign in with the account you use with Office. This can be your Microsoft account, or a work or school account.   Can't remember your account?
  1. Select  Install Office.
    • Having install or sign in trouble, or don't see an option to install Office?  See  Install Office on your PC or Mac and select a step-by-step tab which includes more detailed steps to help you install Office on your PC or Mac.
